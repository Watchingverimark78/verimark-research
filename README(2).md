if you're reading this, you found me.

i worked for verimark global solutions. environmental consulting — 
that's what it said on paper. what it actually was, was a machine 
for making problems disappear. contaminated sites certified clean. 
falsified reports filed with authorities.

i started noticing small discrepancies. numbers that changed 
between the field and the office. things you only see when you're 
standing on the ground looking at them — contamination levels that 
never made it into the final reports, restricted areas with no 
record of why, a perimeter on zone 4 that existed on the ground 
but nowhere on paper.

you don't notice these things until you're looking.
then you can't stop.

i couldn't stay quiet. that's always been my problem.

they're onto me. my computer was accessed.
i've been sleeping somewhere else this week.
i can't keep doing this. they're on my trail.
i'm leaving. i don't know where.

this file is the only real record of me.
the way to unlock it is three things:
the initial of my name.
the address number of the site in the photo.
the year i started investigating.

put them together. no spaces. no symbols.
